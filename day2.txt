Service Plan and Contract Mgmt - Module 7
-----------------------------------------

Applicable Product = Warranty Term + Product

Available Service - Broad - Pricing Rules and Services Offerings
Activity Master - Granular - Specific to a... product?
SLA - Service Level Agreement

Service Plan - roll up of Available Services, Activity Master, SLA

Pricing Rules -> Available Services
Labor Pricing -> Activity Product

Service Offerings: services offered at a discount for x # of units  


Service Contract: Start and End Date for Service Contract, Service Plan, Contact

Service Price Book - ServiceMax object, pricebook for services, activities

Pro-Forma is available Out of the Box
